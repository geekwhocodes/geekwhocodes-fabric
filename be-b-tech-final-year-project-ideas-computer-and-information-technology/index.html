    Some of you thinking of 'what is the difference between AI & ML?' so let me explain it in short. it might be accurate to say that they are two fields attempting to solve very similar problems but with different approaches, you can read more at here.

    -Detecting Devastating Diseases in Search Logs:-

    Web search is the primary resource of information for people concerned about the significance of health-related symptoms. We can explore the promise of harnessing behavioral signals in search logs to provide insights and methodologies that could lead to advance warning about the presence of devastating diseases such as pancreatic adenocarcinoma. Pancreatic adenocarcinoma is often diagnosed too late to be treated eﬀectively as cancer has usually metastasized by the time of diagnosis.
    I'll provide the all papers PDF link later in this post.

    -Urban Water-Quality Prediction based on Multi-task Multi-view Learning:-

    Urban water quality is of great importance to our daily lives. Prediction of urban water quality help control water pollution and protect human health. In this work, we forecast the water quality of a station over the next few hours, using a multitask multi-view learning method to fuse multiple datasets from different domains.

    2. Health Related -

    Everyone knows there are lots of instruments that uses the latest technology to help to diagnose various disease.

    -Patterns of Email Use on Productivity and Stress:-

    While working in well established or small firm many people use Email for communication, it provides numerous benefits but it is unclear patterns of email use might affect user's productivity and stress. We can investigate/track email use patterns: duration, interruption habit, and batching, relate to perceived workplace productivity and stress off-course we need some biosensors as well to identify some biological aspects.

    -Predicting Depression via Social Media :-

    Major depression constitutes a serious challenge in personal and public health. Tens of millions of people each year suffer from depression and only a fraction receives adequate treatment. We explore the potential to use social media (twitter, facebook etc.) to detect and diagnose major depressive disorder in individuals(it's a bit complicated, though ;) ).

    -MoodTracker | Monitoring Collective Emotions in the Workplace:-

    Accurate and timely assessment of collective emotions in the workplace is a critical managerial task. However, perceptual, normative, and methodological challenges make it very difficult even for the most experienced organizational leaders. we can present a MoodTracker(you can name it anything you want) - a technological solution that can help to overcome these challenges and facilitate a continuous monitoring of the collective emotions in large groups in real-time.
